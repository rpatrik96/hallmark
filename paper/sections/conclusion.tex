\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

\textsc{Hallmark} is the first benchmark for BibTeX metadata integrity: 14 hallucination types across 3 tiers, 2,525 entries with 6 sub-tests each, tier-weighted metrics. DOI-only detects 26\%; zero-shot LLMs span 46--88\%, but parametric verification breaks on recent papers (temporal supplement: FPR ${\sim}4{\times}$, F1 near-chance). Code: \url{https://anonymous.4open.science/r/hallmark/}.
