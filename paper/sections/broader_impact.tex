\section{Broader impact}
\label{sec:broader_impact}

\textsc{Hallmark} supports scientific integrity by enabling systematic comparison of citation-verification tools, informing venue deployment as LLM-assisted writing becomes standard.
A taxonomy of hallucination types could theoretically aid adversaries, but the defensive value of understanding these types outweighs the risk; the taxonomy is derived from publicly documented incidents, not novel attack research.
\textsc{Hallmark} is open-source (MIT), pip-installable, GPU-free, and hosted on Hugging Face with Croissant metadata, ensuring access regardless of institutional resources.
